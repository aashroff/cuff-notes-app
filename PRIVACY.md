# CuffNotes Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 March 2026

## Data Collection

CuffNotes does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- No account or login is required
- No personal information is collected
- No analytics or tracking tools are used
- No advertising is displayed
- No data is shared with third parties

## Data Stored on Your Device

Study progress and cached flashcard data are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

## Network Requests

The app makes requests to GitHub (raw.githubusercontent.com) solely to check for updated flashcard content. No personal data is sent.
